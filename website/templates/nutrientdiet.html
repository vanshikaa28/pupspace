Dog Feeding Guides:
  Diet & Nutrition Tips

Your dog's health is dependent on the food and nutrition you provide him.
We'll advise you on your dog's diet–what you should be feeding him and how much–throughout every stage of his life.

Unlike cats which are obligate carnivores, dogs are omnivores which means they can eat a variety of foods.
Healthy nutrition for a dog means that we need to feed them a properly balanced diet of the required nutrients
(different types of nourishing food) every day.
